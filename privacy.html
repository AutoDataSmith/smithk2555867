Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Sepetember 25th, 2025

Information Collection
Midler's Boat House collects personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you make a reservation or contact us through our website, midlerslakehouse.com. We may also collect payment information for booking purposes.
Use of Information
Your information is used to process reservations, communicate with you about your stay, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as needed to provide our services (e.g., payment processors) or as required by law.
Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
Cookies
Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.
Your Rights
You may request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at legal@midlerslakehouse.com. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable laws.
Contact Information
For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Legal Affairs at legal@midlerslakehouse.com or call 111.222.3333.
