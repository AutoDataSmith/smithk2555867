The Main House

Midler Lake House offers a selection of exquisitely appointed accommodations designed for ultimate comfort and privacy. Our historic Grand white Estate features spacious rooms and suites.

Lakeview Suite: Overlooking the shimmering waters of Lake George, this king-sized suite includes a private balcony, en-suite bathroom with jacuzzi tub, and plush seating area. Ideal for honeymoons or anniversaries. Sleeps 2. Starting at $450/night

Adirondack Family Room: A generous space with two queen beds, a pull-out sofa, and views of the surrounding forests. Perfect for family get-togethers. Sleeps up to 6. Starting at $350/night.

Executive Retreat Suite: Tailored for corporate retreats, this suite features a king bed, work desk, high-speed Wi-Fi, and a mini-conference area. Sleeps 2-4. Starting at $400/night.

All accommodations include complimentary breakfast, luxury linens, and access to our full range of amenities. Rates are based on double occupancy and may vary by season. Contact Sidney Stevens for availability and custom packages.

History

The Main House at Midler Lake House, a historic Grand white Estate, boasts a rich legacy that began in the late 19th century when it was built in 1885 as a summer retreat for a prominent Albany merchant family.

Perched on a scenic point lot along Lake George in the Adirondacks, the estate became a cherished gathering place for high society, hosting elegant soirées against the backdrop of the lake’s shimmering waters.

Designed in the classic Victorian style, the house features intricate woodwork, expansive verandas, and a timeless charm that was carefully preserved during its meticulous renovation completed in 2024 by owners Daphne and Timothy Midler.

Restoration

The recent restoration introduced modern luxuries while retaining original elements like the grand staircase and stained-glass windows, making it an ideal venue for upscale family get-togethers, corporate retreats, honeymoons, and anniversaries, steeped in historic elegance.
