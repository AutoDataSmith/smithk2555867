About Us

At Midler's Lake House, we invite you to step into a world of refined serenity and Adirondack splendor. Our story begins with Daphne and Timothy Midler, a devoted middle-aged couple whose passion for hospitality and love for Lake George inspired them to transform this historic Grand white Estate into an upscale Bed & Breakfast.

Our Vision

Daphne, with her background in interior design, and Timothy, a seasoned outdoors enthusiast, purchased the property in 2018. After a comprehensive renovation completed in 2024, the Estate now stands as a beacon of luxury, preserving its original architectural grandeur while incorporating state-of-the-art amenities.
