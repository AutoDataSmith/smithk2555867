View our Great Accomodations

Midler Lake House offers a selection of exquisitely appointed accommodations designed for ultimate comfort and privacy. Our historic Grand white Estate features spacious rooms and suites, while the charming small cottage and boathouse provide unique options for smaller groups or romantic getaways.

All spaces have been recently renovated with high-end finishes, ensuring a blend of classic charm and contemporary luxury.

All accommodations include complimentary breakfast, luxury linens, and access to our full range of amenities. Rates are based on double occupancy and may vary by season. Contact Sidney Stevens for availability and custom packages.
