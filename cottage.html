Our Hideaway Cottage

A standalone one-bedroom cottage with full kitchen, living room, and private deck. Suited for intimate escapes or small families. Sleeps 4. Starting at $500/night.

All accommodations include complimentary breakfast, luxury linens, and access to our full range of amenities. Rates are based on double occupancy and may vary by season. Contact Sidney Stevens for availability and custom packages.

History

The charming cottage at Midler Lake House, nestled amidst lush gardens on the historic Grand white Estate, was originally built in 1902 as a private retreat for the original Albany merchant family’s guests.

This quaint, single-story structure, with its rustic stone facade and cozy interior, was designed to offer intimate seclusion steps from Lake George’s shores.

Restoration

By the late 20th century, the cottage and its surrounding gardens, once vibrant with native wildflowers and manicured hedges, had fallen into disrepair.

In 2024, owners Daphne and Timothy Midler spearheaded a meticulous restoration, reviving the cottage with modern comforts like a gourmet kitchen and updated plumbing while preserving its historic charm.

The gardens were revitalized with native Adirondack flora, creating a serene oasis of colorful blooms and shaded pathways, perfect for upscale guests seeking tranquility during family get-togethers, corporate retreats, honeymoons, and anniversaries.
