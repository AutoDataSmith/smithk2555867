Boating on Lake George

From the private dock of Midler Lake House, guests can embark on unforgettable boating and sailing adventures on the pristine waters of Lake George, the "Queen of American Lakes."

Step directly from our historic Grand white Estate onto our well-equipped boathouse, where complimentary kayaks, paddleboards, and a sleek sailboat await.

Glide across the crystal-clear lake, surrounded by the majestic Adirondack Mountains, as you explore hidden coves, tranquil bays, and scenic shorelines. For a tailored experience, our Property Manager, Sidney Stevens, can arrange guided boating tours or yacht charters, perfect for family outings, corporate team-building, or romantic sunset sails during honeymoons and anniversaries.

Whether you're an experienced sailor or a first-time paddler, the serene beauty and gentle breezes of Lake George offer an idyllic setting for aquatic adventures right from our doorstep.
