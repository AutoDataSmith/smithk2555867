Who dosen't Love Shopping?

Shopping in downtown Lake George, just a short drive from Midler Lake House, offers guests a delightful experience filled with quaint shops and charming attractions perfect for our upscale clientele.

The vibrant Lake George Village is brimming with boutique stores showcasing handcrafted Adirondack keepsakes, artisanal jewelry, and upscale home decor, ideal for finding unique souvenirs or gifts for family get-togethers, corporate retreats, or romantic honeymoons and anniversaries.

Stroll along tree-lined streets to discover local art galleries, cozy bookstores, and specialty shops offering everything from designer clothing to gourmet treats. Nearby attractions, including historic sites and seasonal festivals, add to the charm, while lakeside cafes provide a perfect spot to relax.

Our Property Manager, Sidney Stevens, can recommend the best shopping routes and seasonal events to enhance your visit to this picturesque Adirondack gem.
